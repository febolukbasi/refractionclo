The biggest inspirations for REFRACTION are:

ILLVZN

ILLVZN is a streetwear brand made by two YouTube brothers, Tobjizzle & Manny. Despite the two being YouTubers, the brand has transcended the average plane. They are internationally known and alot of famous people wear it. The quality of these streetwear products is great, even the cheaper pieces of clothing. ILLVZN is a solid brand which has designs that are universally usable and is a great inspiration point. ILLVZN is a brand which can only be compared to other high-quality, premium clothing brands. This is why REFRACTION strives to use them as a big inspiration.

This design of ILLVZN was a great inspiration for our design of "Shards". The simplicity yet qualitative print of the design and the way it looks is why it is a sold out piece of clothing.

Off-White

Off-White is an internationally famous premium, designer clothing brand. Off-White is famous because of the late Virgil Abloh as well as the classic Off-White text and the famous X-arrows. The quality of this brand is like none other, it surpasses normal designs and likes to be unique with their designs. Off-White is also famous because of their iconic tag on the shoes. Most Off-White shoes also sell for $2000+ which shows the quality and design. Off-White is a designer brand with a signature design, which REFRACTION strives to also have.

This design of Off-White was a great inspiration for our design of "Refract". It is a very simple design, with a simple text with "OFF" in the middle. This shows that not all designs have to be crowded to look good.

Internet.Made

Internet.Made is a brand made by a famous YouTuber called Zerkaa. The brand was called ZRK Clothing first but to broaden their community they rebranded to Internet.Made. The brand stands for the roots of Zerkaa and tells us that you shouldn't forget your beginnings. The brand has high-quality clothing and uses high gsm which means it is of great quality.

This design of Internet.Made is a very simple design but uses alot of design principles. It centralises the logo and adds a little text to compliment the logo. It's a simple yet layered design which REFRACTION takes good inspiration from.
